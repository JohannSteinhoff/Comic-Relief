Who we are

A Local Legend on Aquarena Springs

Comic Relief is San Marcos's home for cards, comics and toys — the kind of shop where you can lose an afternoon flipping through long boxes and walk out with a key issue, a graded rookie card, and a vintage figure you forgot you needed.

Whether you're a lifelong collector, a Magic player hunting singles, or just discovering your first pull list, you'll find friendly faces and shelves stacked floor-to-ceiling with the good stuff.

  • Weekly new comic releases
  • Buy · sell · trade
  • Magic: The Gathering & trading card singles
  • Vintage & modern toys and collectibles
